Blade

Summary

The blade occupies the top 60 pixels of every page and contains "Quick Links" to commonly used pages and a search box. On pages that are not the ualberta.ca institutional pages, the University of Alberta logo sits at the left position of the blade.

Devices

Mobile

Quick Links collapse to a 3 bar icon and search collapses to a magnifying glass icon which expands on :focus.

Tablet

Quick links collapse into a button labelled "Quick Links". Search remains unchanged.

Desktop

Quick links are fully expanded and search form has a button.

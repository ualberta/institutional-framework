Faculty Footer

Summary

On faculty pages, an extra row exists on the footer to display some faculty specific links, faculty social media buttons, an faculty contact information. The faculty footer row appears above the University of Alberta footer.

Components

  • Faculty Links: Component description.
  • Faculty Social Media: Component description.
  • Faculty Contact Information: Component description.

Devices

Mobile

Faculty links, social media, and contact information stack vertically on top of each other.

Tablet

Faculty links and social media are grouped on the left and contact information appears on the right.

Desktop

Faculty links and social media are grouped on the left and contact information appears on the right.

Variants

  • Institutional Footer: The faculty footer does not display on institutional pages.

Banner

Summary

The banner appears directly below the blade and provides the identity part of the website.

Components

  • Logo: The University of Alberta logo is the only logo allowed inside of the banner. It is only displayed on the institutional variant.
  • Identifier: The identifier text is used on department sites to establish which faculty a department belongs to. The identifier is used on the institutional variant and the faculty variant pages.
  • Site Title: The site title is where the primary title of the site is located. On Faculty sites, the faculty name is the site title. On department sites, the department name is the site title.
  • Audience Navigation: Audience navigation is currently only used for the ualberta.ca institutional pages

Devices

Mobile

On mobile, the audience navigation disappears and is added to the global navigation dropdown.

Tablet

The audience navigation moves into two columns of links.

Desktop

The logo or identity piece is displayed on the left side of the banner, and on institutional pages the audience navigation appears on the right side of the banner.

Variants

  • Faculties: Faculties will display their faculty name in the identity area of the banner. Faculties do not commonly have audience navigation.
  • Departments: Departments will display their department name following the faculty identifier. Departments do not commonly have audience navigation.
  • Institutional: Institutional pages show the University of Alberta logo on the left of the banner, and audience navigation on the right.

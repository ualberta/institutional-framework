Global Navigation

Summary

The global navigation for the website sits directly below the banner. The global navigation will have somewhere between 4 and 6 items that span 100% of the container width.

Components

  • Menu Items: Provide navigation to deeper levels of the website. Where required, a sub menu will display after hovering over a menu item.

Devices

Mobile

Navigation behaves as an accordion toggle. When the user selects "show navigation", the top level and audience navigation elements are displayed.

Tablet

Global navigation switches to fluid and takes up 100% of the window width.

Desktop

Global navigation fills 100% of the containing element. Navigation items are have widths proportional to their size.

Variants

  • Faculties: For consistency across the University, faculty navigation will have the navigation items: About us, Programs, Research, Departments. In addition to these 4 navigation items, the faculty may choose two additional navigation items.
  • Departments: Departments are still being integrated to the framework. Navigation details are still being developed.
  • Institutional: Institutional pages will have the following navigational items: Why UAlberta?, Faculties & Programs, Research, Admissions, Campus Life, News & Events

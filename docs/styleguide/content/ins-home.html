Institutional Homepage

Summary

The ualberta.ca homepage follows a three column layout. The feature messaging area and why ualberta space spans the entire width of the 3 columns. The explore bar takes up the width of the primary and secondary columns, with the sidebar on the right. The news appears in the primary content area under the explore bar. The campus notices and colloquy blog feeds are displayed in the secondary content space next to the news. Lastly, the twitter feed and social media links are displayed in the sidebar.

Flexible Messaging Area

Summary

The flexible messaging area is used to convey important messages about upcoming events, deadlines, and activities happening at the University. Special consideration needs to be taken when selecting images, as they need to work with the content on all devices.

Properties
  • Width: 320px - 1170px
  • Height: 350px
Allowed Components
  • Custom HTML
  • Image w/ Caption
  • Video
  • Carousel

Why UAlberta Area

Summary

The Why UAlberta space is used to inform the user why UAlberta is a good choice for them, and provides a way to get to the most important areas of the University website.

Properties
  • Width: 320px - 1170px
  • Height: ~600px - 180px
Allowed Components
  • Custom HTML

Explore Bar

Summary

The explore area consists of UAlberta's most sought after links represented by a series of icons paired with text. The links can be navigated through the directional arrows or by swiping left or right on a mobile device.

Properties
  • Width: 320px - 970px
  • Height: 125px
Allowed Components
  • Explore Bar

Primary Content Area

Summary

The primary content area is designated for important information that will appeal to a wide audience. Currently this space is devoted to news headlines and teasers.

Properties
  • Width: 320px - 580px
  • Height: Depends on content
Allowed Components
  • News headlines w/ teaser

Secondary Content Area

Summary

The secondary content area is a space to put content that is not crucial to the page but serves some purpose to the user. On the homepage the secondary content pieces are the campus notices and colloquy blog.

Properties
  • Width: 320px - 722px
  • Height: Depends on content
Allowed Components
  • Campus Notices Feed
  • Colloquy Blog Feed

Sidebar

Summary

The sidebar area is reserved for content that is not directly related to the page's content, but may provide of some use to the user.

Properties
  • Width: 170px - 670px

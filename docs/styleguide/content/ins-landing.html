Institutional Landing Page

Summary

The ualberta.ca homepage follows a three column layout. The feature messaging area and why ualberta space spans the entire width of the 3 columns. The explore bar takes up the width of the primary and secondary columns, with the sidebar on the right. The news appears in the primary content area under the explore bar. The campus notices and colloquy blog feeds are displayed in the secondary content space next to the news. Lastly, the twitter feed and social media links are displayed in the sidebar.

Flexible Messaging Area (Optional)

Summary

The flexible messaging area is used to convey important messages relating to the content and goals of the page. Special consideration needs to be taken when selecting images, as they need to work with the content on all devices.

Properties
  • Width: 320px - 1170px
  • Height: 350px
Allowed Components
  • Custom HTML
  • Image w/ Caption
  • Video
  • Carousel

Primary Content Area

Summary

The primary content area is designated for content that will contribute to achieving the goals that have been set for the page.

Properties
  • Width: 320px - 580px
  • Height: Depends on content
Allowed Components
  • Categorized Link Bucket
  • Rich Text
  • Custom HTML

  • Video
  • Media Items (Data List)

Secondary Content Area

Summary

The secondary content area is a space to put content that is not crucial to the page but is related to the page's topic/goals and serves a purpose to the user.

Properties
  • Width: 320px - 722px
  • Height: Depends on content
Allowed Components
  • Feed
  • Video
  • Rich Text
  • Custom HTML
  • Inline Form

Sidebar

Summary

The sidebar area is reserved for content that is not directly related to the page's content, but may provide of some use to the user.

Properties

  • Height: Depends on content

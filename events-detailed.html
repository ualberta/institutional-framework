Toronto U of A Alumni Grey Cup Party

Nov 25, 2012 4:30 AM - 9:00 PM Turf Lounge, 330 Bay Street, Toronto ON M5H 4A6

Add event to Google Calendar | View on Campus Map

Join your fellow University of Alberta alumni for an afternoon of great football, and a chance to win tickets to the game! All University of Alberta alumni who register before November 21st will be ENTERED TO WIN a pair of silver tickets to the Grey Cup! Tickets will be drawn at the event at 5:15 sharp, and you must be present to win.

Registration: 4:30 pm
Draw: 5:15 pm

Game time: 6:00 pm Location: Turf Lounge
330 Bay Street, Toronto
www.turflounge.com
Cost: $15 (includes a complimentary beverage and a variety of appetizers).
Register before Wednesday, November 21 for a chance to win 2 Grey Cup tickets!
